♠ Privacy Policy

Devil's Ace — Blackjack Practice Coach (browser extension). Last updated: 2026-07-01.

Summary: This extension collects nothing, sends nothing, and has no servers. Everything runs locally in your browser.

What we collect

Nothing. The extension does not collect, store off-device, transmit, sell, or share any personal or usage data. There is no analytics, no tracking, no account, and no network communication of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your chosen settings (counting system and table rules) are saved locally using the browser's chrome.storage.sync API so they persist between sessions and, if you're signed into your browser, sync across your own devices via your browser vendor. Your current count during a session lives only in the page's memory and is discarded when you close or reload. None of this is ever sent to us — we have no way to receive it.

Camera / page content

This extension does not use your camera and does not read, scrape, or transmit the content of the pages you visit. It only injects a self-contained overlay (a count HUD and strategy coach) into the tab when you explicitly activate it, using the activeTab permission. You enter cards manually; the extension does the math.

Permissions

  • activeTab — to show the overlay only on the tab you explicitly activate it on.
  • scripting — to inject the overlay's code when you click the toolbar button or press the shortcut.
  • storage — to save your system/rule preferences locally.

Intended use

This is an educational practice/training tool. It is not intended for use during real-money gambling, which may violate gambling operators' terms of service and may be unlawful in your jurisdiction.
